HIRAYA FOUNDATION COLLEGE SCHOLARSHIP

      The Hiraya Foundation College Scholarship Program aims to provide financial assistance and academic resources to students demonstrating dedication and potential, enabling access to higher education, encouraging academic excellence, and transforming sustained effort into meaningful academic and personal achievements.

Eligibility Criteria

Educational Background

Grade 12 students graduating in School Year 2025–2026, or Grade 12 graduates prior to SY 2025–2026 who have not yet enrolled in college

Citizenship

Must be a natural-born Filipino citizen

Character & Health

Must be of good moral character and in good health

Academic Performance

Must have a General Weighted Average (GWA) of at least 90% or its equivalent for Grade 11

Courses Offered

Technology, Engineering, and Architecture

  • BS in Computer Science
  • BS in Information Technology
  • BS in Civil Engineering
  • BS in Computer Engineering
  • BS in Electronics Engineering
  • BS in Mechanical Engineering
  • BS in Architecture

Social Sciences, Education, and Business and Management

  • BS in Psychology
  • BS in Elementary Education
  • BS in Secondary Education
  • BS in Business Administration
  • BS in Accountancy
  • BS in Entrepreneurship

Documents to Prepare

  1. Proof of Enrollment (Certificate of Registration / COR): Shows current enrollment or intended college program.
  2. Valid ID or School ID: For identity verification.
  3. Grade 12 Report Card: Shows academic performance and eligibility.
  4. PSA Birth Certificate: Confirms date of birth and Filipino citizenship.
  5. Certificate of Good Moral: Confirms applicant's good conduct from school authorities.
  6. Certificate of Good Health: Confirms the applicant is physically fit.
  7. Certificate of Indigency: Provides proof of financial need.
  8. Recommendation Letter(s): From teachers or school authorities endorsing the applicant.
