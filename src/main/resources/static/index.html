WHO WE ARE

Hiraya Foundation is a Filipino non-profit organization dedicated to nurturing dreams and empowering the next generation. We strive to create opportunities that help young Filipinos reach their full potential and make a positive impact on society.

At the heart of our work is the Hiraya Foundation College Scholarship Program, which provides deserving students with financial support, mentorship, and resources to excel academically. Our scholarships recognize talent, perseverance, and potential, helping scholars turn their dreams into reality.

OUR PARTNER SCHOOLS

The scholarship program collaborates with selected colleges and universities across the Philippines to provide students with opportunities for higher education. Partner institutions include a mix of state universities, private universities, and other recognized schools, ensuring access to quality programs for eligible scholars.
