WHO WE ARE

Hiraya Foundation is a Filipino non-profit organization dedicated to nurturing dreams and empowering the next generation. We strive to create opportunities that help young Filipinos reach their full potential and make a positive impact on society. At the heart of our work is the Hiraya Foundation College Scholarship Program, which provides deserving students with financial support, mentorship, and resources to excel academically. Our scholarships recognize talent, perseverance, and potential, helping scholars turn their dreams into reality.

WHAT WE OFFER

  • Monthly Stipend: Scholars receive a monthly allowance to help cover school and living expenses.
  • Internship Opportunities: Hands-on experience to build skills and explore career paths.
  • Conferences & Seminars: Opportunities to expand knowledge and connections.

OUR IMPACT

  • 6,543 scholarship grants awarded
  • 4,321 scholar graduates
  • 789 scholar graduates with Latin honors
  • 1,234 current scholars
